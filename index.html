Built for Security

Every feature designed with enterprise-level security in mind

🔐

AES-256 Encryption

Military-grade encryption protects your vault. Data is encrypted locally before any sync. Even we can't read your passwords.

🙋

Biometric Unlock

Fingerprint or face unlock for instant access. Hardware-backed Keystore ensures biometric keys never leave the secure enclave.

🔄

Enterprise Sync

Sync passwords across your team securely. End-to-end encrypted sync with your organization's hCRM server.

🛠

Android Autofill

One-tap login in any app or browser. Inline suggestions in the keyboard strip with smart domain matching.

🕒

TOTP Authenticator

Built-in 2FA code generator. No separate authenticator app needed — OTP codes right in your vault.

📱

Tablet Optimized

Adaptive layout for phones, foldables, and tablets. Master-detail split view on wide screens for productivity.
